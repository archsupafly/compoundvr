# Oldest Flat-to-VR Mods Chronology

**Research Date:** March 19, 2026
**Status:** In Progress

---

## Definition

This list focuses on **game-specific VR mods** (not generic injection drivers like VorpX/Vireio). We're looking for the first dedicated mods that brought VR support to specific flat games.

---

## Injection Drivers (Framework Category — Excluded from Main List)

These are frameworks, not game-specific mods:

| Name | First Release | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| **Vireio Perception** | October 8, 2012 | First VR injection driver, predates consumer headsets. Targeted Oculus DK1. Free, open source. |
| **VorpX** | ~2013 (exact date unclear) | Commercial injection driver. Became most well-known. Still actively maintained. |
| **TriDef 3D (VR support)** | Pre-2014 | Stereoscopic injector with HMD support. Discontinued. |

## Research Notes

### Doom 3 BFG VR: Fully Possessed (March 2017)
- GitHub: https://github.com/KozGit/DOOM-3-BFG-VR
- First alpha (v0.020) released March 3, 2017
- v0.021 released March 12, 2017
- Native VR implementation: motion controls, room-scale, voice commands
- Based on RBDOOM-3-BFG port
- Not to be confused with Doom3Quest (Quest standalone) or official Doom 3 VR Edition

### MotherVR (July 2017)
- GitHub: https://github.com/Nibre/MotherVR
- First alpha v0.1.0 released July 30, 2017
- Development ongoing (v0.8.1 as of 2020)
- Restores VR to Alien: Isolation (official VR demo shown at E3 2014 but never released)
- Motion controls added in v0.8.0

### Half-Life 2 VR Mod
- Development began in early DK1/DK2 era (~2012-2013)
- Source VR Mod Team
- Full release (HL2 + Episodes) September 2022
- Steam distribution
