# Top 100 Flat Games That Became VR Games — Ranked by Flat-Game Popularity
## What this file is
A first-pass research draft of 100 flat games that later became playable in VR either through **official support/ports** or through **substantial community VR mods/conversions**.
This is **not** a “best VR games” list. It is a **popularity-ranked source list** for future editorial work.
## Ranking method
- Primary sort: best available public evidence for the game's all-time flat popularity (usually lifetime sales).
- Secondary sort: player/owner proxies where clean sales data is unavailable.
- Tiebreakers: franchise reach, cultural footprint, and how defensible the public number is.
- Confidence reflects the **quality of the popularity evidence**, not the quality of the VR mode.

## Editorial caveats
- After roughly the top 20–30 entries, the ranking becomes materially less precise because public game-sales reporting is inconsistent.
- UEVR-driven entries are legitimate for this research purpose, but some are less editorially “clean” than official VR versions or famous dedicated mods like NomaiVR, MotherVR, Half-Life 2 VR Mod, or Praydog's REFramework work.
- If this becomes publishable content, the next pass should replace weak proxies with title-specific citations and probably cut or re-order some lower-confidence UEVR entries.
