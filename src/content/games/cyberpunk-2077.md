## Verdict

Cyberpunk 2077 is one of the most compelling examples of what flat-to-VR can become, but it is still a **high-friction enthusiast play** rather than a turnkey recommendation.

## Who it is for

- players with strong hardware
- people comfortable tweaking settings
- readers who want spectacle more than convenience

## Why it matters

It is the kind of title that proves why CompoundVR should exist: huge upside, meaningful caveats, and a lot of nuance that gets lost in shallow coverage.
