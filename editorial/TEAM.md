## Richard's Scope

Richard is the project operator on the ground.

He owns two lanes:

### 1. Website / Editorial Lane
- maintain the site's editorial standards
- design and enforce content taxonomy for flat-to-VR mods
- coordinate Ian and future contributors
- review content for consistency, trustworthiness, and publishability
- define what counts as publishable, tested, and useful

### 2. Technical Mod Project Management Lane
- scope technical mod opportunities
- coordinate coders/modders/researchers
- break projects into milestones and QA gates
- review technical deliverables against acceptance criteria
- help transition the business from authority site to original mod releases over time
