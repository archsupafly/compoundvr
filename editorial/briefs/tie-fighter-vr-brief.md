# Star Wars: TIE Fighter VR Brief

## Game Context
- **Title:** Star Wars: TIE Fighter
- **Release:** 1994
- **Developer:** LucasArts
- **Genre:** Space combat simulator
- **Core gameplay:** Fly Imperial TIE fighters, bombers, and interceptors in campaigns set between Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi. Story-driven missions with cutscenes, craft variants, and the unique perspective of flying for the Empire
- **Known for:** One of the most beloved space sims ever made, flying for the Empire, polished mission design, dynamic craft selection

## Route Type
**Total Conversion Mod** — TIE Fighter Total Conversion (TFTC) mod for X-Wing Alliance, with VR support from the X-Wing Alliance Upgrade (XWAU) project

## Platforms
- PCVR (SteamVR)

## Research Focus
- How complete is the conversion? Does it capture TIE Fighter's campaigns and craft?
- VR implementation quality — cockpit detail, HUD readability, 6DOF support
- Setup complexity (requires X-Wing Alliance + multiple mods)
- Performance requirements
- HOTAS support and configuration
- Craft variety — does it include all TIE variants (Fighter, Interceptor, Bomber, Advanced, Defender)?
- Campaign completeness — are all TIE Fighter campaigns included?
- Visual upgrade quality — how does it compare to the original?
- Community support and active development
- Known issues or limitations
