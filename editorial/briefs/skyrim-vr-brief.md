## Route Type Context

Official Standalone VR Version — native VR port, designed for VR, sold as separate VR product. Built by Bethesda, supported on SteamVR and PSVR.

---

## Game-Specific Context

Open-world RPG from Bethesda Game Studios, originally released 2011. Skyrim VR is a full VR port of the Special Edition, released November 2017 for PSVR, April 2018 for PCVR. Includes base game + all three DLC expansions (Dawnguard, Hearthfire, Dragonborn). Full open-world exploration, combat, crafting, and questing in VR with motion controls.

---

## Multiplatform Note

Two primary platforms:
- **PCVR (SteamVR):** Higher visual fidelity, mod support (including VR-specific mods), requires VR-capable PC
- **PSVR (PS4/PS5):** Lower visual quality, console simplicity, no mod support, more accessible
