## Route Type Context

---

## Game-Specific Context

Minecraft (2011) is the best-selling video game of all time. Created by Markus Persson, later developed by Mojang/Microsoft. Sandbox survival/crafting with procedurally generated worlds. Multiple VR paths exist: Quest native Bedrock edition, Windows 10 Bedrock with official VR toggle, and Vivecraft for Java Edition with community development since 2016. Vivecraft offers the most comprehensive motion control implementation. Quest version is the most accessible standalone option.

---

## Multiplatform Note

Three main VR paths:
1. **Quest Standalone** — Bedrock Edition, native Quest app, motion controls, seated/standing, limited mods
2. **PCVR Bedrock** — Windows 10 Edition with VR toggle, requires VR-capable PC, Bedrock features (cross-play with console/mobile)
3. **Vivecraft (Java Edition)** — Community mod, requires PC + VR, most complete motion control implementation, compatible with Java mods and shaders

Research all three as distinct experiences with different pros/cons.
