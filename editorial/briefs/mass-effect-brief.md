## Route Type Context

Injection Driver: VorpX stereoscopic injection. Provides 3D depth and head tracking only. No motion controls, no hand presence. Gamepad or KBM required.

## Game-Specific Context

Mass Effect (2007) is BioWare's landmark sci-fi RPG that launched the trilogy. Third-person shooter/cover mechanics with deep dialogue trees and squad management. One of the most acclaimed RPGs of its generation.

VorpX support is the only VR route. No official VR port exists. No community VR mod exists (the game uses Unreal Engine 3, which has limited VR modding potential).
