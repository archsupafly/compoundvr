# Battlezone VR (2016) Brief

## Game Context
- **Title:** Battlezone (2016)
- **Release:** October 2016 (PSVR launch title), 2017 for PC
- **Developer:** Rebellion Developments
- **Genre:** First-person tank combat, VR-exclusive
- **Core gameplay:** Pilot the Cobra tank through neon-scarred sci-fi landscapes, first-person tank combat against AI drones and tanks, campaign with co-op support
- **Known for:** PSVR launch title, built-from-ground-up VR tank combat, Classic Mode that emulates the 1980 original

## Route Type
**Official Standalone VR Version** — Native VR game built specifically for VR, not a flat game port

## Platforms
- PSVR (PlayStation VR)
- PCVR (SteamVR)
- Also available on Xbox One and Switch (flat, non-VR versions)
