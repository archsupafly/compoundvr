## Game-Specific Context

Until Dawn: Rush of Blood is an on-rails VR horror shooter developed by Supermassive Games, released in 2016 as a PlayStation VR launch title. It's a spinoff of the 2015 narrative horror game Until Dawn, but unlike the main game, Rush of Blood is an arcade-style rail shooter where players sit in a rollercoaster cart and shoot enemies with PS Move controllers while riding through horror-themed environments.
