# Ace Combat 7 VR Brief

**Game:** Ace Combat 7: Skies Unknown
**Platforms:** PC (Steam)
**Research Focus:**
- What VR route exists for this game (mod, injection driver, official support, etc.)
- Setup complexity and user experience
- Flight stick/HOTAS support and compatibility
- Cockpit visibility and HUD readability
- Comfort issues (motion sickness in high-speed flight)
- Performance demands and hardware requirements
- Whether the VR experience justifies the friction

**Output:** First draft at `/editorial/drafts/ace-combat-7-draft.md`

**Context:**
- Flight sims benefit enormously from head tracking, but VR implementation quality varies wildly
- Key question: Does whatever VR solution exists add enough to justify the effort?
