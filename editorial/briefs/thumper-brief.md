## Route Type Context

This is an **Official Hybrid** — a flat game that launched with VR support built-in. Same game, playable in VR or on a monitor. Toggle between modes. This is different from "Official Standalone VR Version" (VR-first) and different from "Full VR Mod" (community-added VR).

---

## Game-Specific Context

- Released October 13, 2016 (same day as PSVR launch)
- "Rhythm violence" game — high-speed, aggressive, visceral
- Developed by Drool (two-person team)
- Published by Devolver Digital
- VR optional from launch — entire game playable flat or VR
- On-rails rhythm game where you control a beetle traveling down a track
- Minimalist aesthetic: dark, geometric, pulsing visuals
- Brutal difficulty, no failure state (restart checkpoints)
- About 2-3 hours for main playthrough
- Available on: PSVR, PS4, PC (Steam), Switch, Quest, Xbox One
