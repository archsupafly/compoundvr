# Duck Hunt VR Brief

## Game Context
- **Title:** Duck Hunt
- **Release:** April 1984 (NES)
- **Developer:** Nintendo
- **Genre:** Light gun shooter
- **Core gameplay:** Use the NES Zapper to shoot ducks as they fly across the screen. A dog retrieves fallen ducks and laughs at your misses. Simple, iconic, frustrating.
- **Known for:** The laughing dog meme, light gun peripheral, bundled with Super Mario Bros. in the NES Action Set

## Route Type
**3dSen VR** — Real-time 2D-to-3D voxel transformation with motion controller support

## Platforms
- PCVR (SteamVR)
- Meta Quest (via Quest Link / Virtual Desktop)

## Research Focus
