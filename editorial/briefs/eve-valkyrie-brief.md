## Game-Specific Context

- Multiplayer space dogfighting game from CCP Games
- Launched March 2016 as Oculus Rift launch title (bundled with headset)
- Originally VR-only for 16 months
- **Warzone update (September 2017):** Added non-VR play on PC and PS4, cross-play between VR and non-VR players
- Designed from the ground up for VR: cockpit visibility, head-tracking targeting, spatial audio
- One of VR's first "AAA" launch titles — significant historical importance
- Still playable but player population is low

---

## Historical Note

EVE: Valkyrie is historically significant as one of VR's first high-profile exclusives. The Warzone update is notable for making VR and non-VR players play together — early example of "cross-reality" gaming. Worth mentioning in the article but don't make it the focus.
