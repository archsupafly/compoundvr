# F.E.A.R. VR Brief

## Game Context
- **Title:** F.E.A.R. (First Encounter Assault Recon)
- **Release:** October 2005
- **Developer:** Monolith Productions
- **Genre:** First-person shooter, horror
- **Core gameplay:** Tactical FPS with supernatural horror elements. Atmospheric corridor combat, slow-motion "reflex" mechanic, horror set pieces featuring ghost girl Alma
- **Known for:** Excellent enemy AI, atmospheric horror, slow-mo gunfights, Alma encounters
