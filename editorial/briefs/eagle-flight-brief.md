## Game-Specific Context

Eagle Flight is a VR-native flight simulation game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in late 2016. Players control an eagle flying through a post-apocalyptic Paris, 50 years after humanity's extinction. The game features single-player exploration and multiplayer aerial combat (3v3 Capture the Prey mode). It was a launch window title for PlayStation VR and supported Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, and PSVR.

Key facts for research:
- Developer: Ubisoft Montreal
- Publisher: Ubisoft
- Release: October 2016 (PCVR), November 2016 (PSVR)
- Composer: Inon Zur
- Genres: Simulation, racing
- Modes: Single-player, multiplayer
