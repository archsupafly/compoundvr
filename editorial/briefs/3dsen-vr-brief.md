# 3dSen VR Brief

## Software Context
- **Title:** 3dSen VR
- **Developer:** geod (solo developer)
- **Release:** v1.0 June 19, 2025 (exited Early Access after 10+ years development)
- **Price:** $24.99 (Steam, itch.io)
- **Platform:** PCVR (SteamVR), Windows/Linux/macOS
- **Type:** Commercial NES emulator with real-time 2D-to-3D transformation

## What Makes It Unique
3dSen VR is **not** a typical VR emulator. It doesn't just play NES games in a virtual room — it **transforms 2D NES games into 3D voxel dioramas in real-time**. This is a fundamental reimagining of the games, not just a VR wrapper.

## Key Features
- Real-time 2D-to-3D voxel conversion
- Hand-made profiles for supported games
- Works with classic NES games and homebrew
- Room-scale VR experience (play inside the game world)
- Supports both classic and modern control schemes
