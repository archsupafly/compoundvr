# Dead Space VR Brief

## Game Context
- **Title:** Dead Space
- **Release:** October 2008
- **Developer:** EA Redwood Shores (now Visceral Games)
- **Genre:** Survival horror, third-person shooter
- **Core gameplay:** Engineer Isaac Clarke fights through necromorph-infected spaceship USG Ishimura, strategic dismemberment combat, atmospheric horror, minimal HUD design
- **Known for:** Innovative dismemberment combat, immersive UI (health on suit, no traditional HUD), sound design, claustrophobic corridors, jump scares

## Route Type
**Injection Driver (VorpX)** — VorpX profile with Geometry 3D support

## Platforms
- PCVR (SteamVR, Oculus via VorpX)

## Research Focus
- How well does VorpX handle third-person camera in VR?
- Dismemberment combat — does the strategic limb-targeting work with mouse/controller?
- Horror intensity in VR — corridor jumpscares amplified?
- UI integration — health bar on Isaac's back, does it work in VR?
- Comfort considerations (horror + third-person camera movement)
- Performance and setup difficulty
- Any known VorpX profile settings or community recommendations
- Comparison to Dead Space Remake (2023) VR support

## Notes
- Dead Space is frequently cited as one of the BEST VorpX experiences due to its corridor design, atmospheric lighting, and third-person camera that works surprisingly well in VR
- The 2023 Remake has no official VR support, but some users may conflate the two — be clear this is about the 2008 original
- Third-person camera in VR is unusual — research how VorpX handles it
- Horror intensity will be amplified in VR — comfort warning
- The dismemberment mechanic (cutting off limbs) is core to gameplay — test if it works naturally
