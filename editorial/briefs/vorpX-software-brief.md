## What VorpX Is

VorpX is a paid ($40) VR injection driver that lets you play flat (non-VR) games in stereoscopic 3D on PCVR headsets. It's not a mod, not native VR — it intercepts the game's rendering and forces stereoscopic output.

**What it provides:**
- Stereoscopic 3D (two images, one per eye)
- Head tracking (look around naturally)
- Sometimes auto-calibration (DirectVR for supported games)
- Virtual cinema modes for games that don't support 3D

**What it does NOT provide:**
- Motion controls (you use gamepad/keyboard/mouse)
- Hand presence
- VR-optimized UI
- Comfort features (teleport, vignette)
- A transformed experience
