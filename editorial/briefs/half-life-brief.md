## VR Options to Cover

### Primary VR Paths

1. **Lambda1VR** (Quest Standalone)
   - Complete VR port of original Half-Life
   - Quest/Pico standalone, no PC required
   - Full motion controls, room-scale
   - Based on GoldSrc engine source leak
   - Most accessible way to play HL1 in VR
   - Free, requires sideloading or third-party store

2. **Half-Life: Source VR Mod**
   - Half-Life running on Source engine
   - PCVR only
   - Motion controls via community mod

3. **Black Mesa VR** (Half-Life remake)
   - Fan-made remake of Half-Life on Source engine
   - VR mod available
   - More modern visuals, updated mechanics
   - Longer development, more polished presentation
   - PCVR only

4. **Half-Life: Alyx** (related context)
   - Not Half-Life 1, but VR-native prequel
   - Worth mentioning as comparison point for VR quality expectations
   - Shows what Valve's own VR vision looks like
