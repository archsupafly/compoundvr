## Game-Specific Context

- Resident Evil 7 biohazard (2017) — first-person survival horror
- PSVR version: included with PS4 version, requires PS4 + PSVR
- One of the first major AAA horror games with full VR support at launch
- Complete game playable in VR — full campaign, all content
- Designed for VR from the ground up (the flat game was designed with VR in mind)
- Horror game: isolated plantation, Baker family, survival horror mechanics
- One of the most celebrated VR horror experiences on PSVR
- Capcom's official support — not a mod
- Uses DualShock 4 controller (no Move support)
