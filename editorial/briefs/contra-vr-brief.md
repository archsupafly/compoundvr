## Route Type Context

3dSen VR is a real-time 2D-to-3D voxel transformation engine that converts NES ROMs into stereoscopic VR experiences. The original sprites remain flat, but environments gain depth, creating a diorama-like play space. No motion controls — standard gamepad input required.

---

## Game-Specific Context

Contra (1986) is Konami's seminal run-and-gun action game, originally an arcade release and later ported to NES. Known for its punishing difficulty, two-player simultaneous co-op, and the iconic spread gun power-up. The game popularized the run-and-gun genre on NES and remains one of the most recognizable titles in the library. 3dSen VR's voxel transformation adds depth perspective to the side-scrolling stages, potentially enhancing the already-intense action.
