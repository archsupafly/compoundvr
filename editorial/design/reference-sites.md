## What to Look For

### Design Research
- Typography scale and hierarchy
- Color systems (light vs dark themes)
- Card/component patterns
- Hero image treatments
- Navigation structures
- Mobile adaptations

### Content Research
- Review structure and length
- Headline conventions
- Score/rating presentation
- Metadata display
- Comparison formats
- Guide/tutorial structure

### Tone/Voice Research
- Level of technical detail
- Enthusiasm vs skepticism balance
- Community vs editorial voice
- First-person vs third-person
- Humor usage

## Anti-References (What to Avoid)

| Site | Why Avoid |
|------|-----------|
| Generic affiliate blogs | Thin content, SEO manipulation, low trust |
| YouTube-first properties | Different medium, different constraints |
| Fan wikis | Unedited, inconsistent, unreliable |
| Reddit (as primary) | Unverified, chaotic, not authoritative |
