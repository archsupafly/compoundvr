## Research Summary

### Game Info
- **Title:** DiRT Rally
- **Developer:** Codemasters
- **Publisher:** Codemasters
- **Original Release:** April 2015 (PC Early Access), full release December 2015
- **VR Release:** July 11, 2016 (Oculus Rift), February 2017 (PSVR)
- **Genre:** Rally racing simulation
- **Platforms:** PC (Oculus Home, Steam), PS4 (PSVR)

### VR Implementation Type
**Official VR Support** - Not a mod. Native VR integration by Codemasters.

### VR Platform Availability
- **Oculus Rift:** Native support via Oculus Home and Steam (July 2016)
- **PSVR:** Native support via $13 DLC upgrade or $43 bundle (February 2017)
- **HTC Vive:** Initially not supported, later added via SteamVR/OpenVR
- **Quest:** No native port (PCVR streaming only)

### Key VR Features
1. **Full cockpit immersion** - Authentic rally car interiors with working instruments
2. **Head tracking** - Look around corners, check mirrors naturally
3. **Scale and depth** - Sense of speed and terrain is dramatically enhanced
4. **Co-Driver Mode (PSVR exclusive)** - Second player acts as navigator using TV
5. **Force feedback wheel support** - Works with VR for full sim experience

### Notable Details
- Considered one of the best VR racing sims (Road to VR review, 2016)
- Serious rally simulation, not arcade-style
- Physics engine includes tyre and surface deformation
- Rally, Rallycross, and Pikes Peak hillclimb events
- FIA World Rallycross Championship licensed content
- Career mode with progression through rally classes

### Performance Considerations
- Requires force-feedback wheel for optimal experience
- Works on potato PCs due to 2015 engine
- Steam version requires Oculus runtime for initial VR support
- Later SteamVR update added Vive/OpenVR support
